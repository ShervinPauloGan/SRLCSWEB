ST. ROSE OF LIMA CATHOLIC SCHOOL

About St. Rose of Lima Catholic School

St. Rose of Lima Catholic School is a faith-based educational institution focused on academic excellence and the moral development of students. Located in Poblacion Santa Rosa Nueva Ecija, the school integrates Catholic teachings into its curriculum, fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment.

Vision

St. Rose of Lima Catholic School is an educational community inspired by the sprirituality of St. Rose of Lima, centered in the person, life and mission of Jesus Christ committed to a transformative and holistic Catholic Education towards the fullness of life.

Mission

  • Boldly promote the living out of Catholic faith and identity.
  • Sustainably nurture a school community in the spirit of servant leadership.
  • Vigorously focus on holistic learner development
  • Innovatively provide opportunities for value-added services and programs that foster faith-filled learning environment.
  • Decisively uphold Christian stewardship of resources for institutional advancement and viability.
